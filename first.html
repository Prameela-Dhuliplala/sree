About Us

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT) is an engineering college located in Gandipet, near Financial District, Hyderabad, Telangana, India. The college was affiliated to Osmania University from 1979 to 2012 and accredited by the National Board of Accreditation. The institute received an autonomous status in 2013. It has an history of 39 years.CBIT was established in 1979. The institute started with three undergraduate programmes with an intake of 200 students. By 2014 this had grown to nine undergraduate and seven postgraduate courses with a total intake of 9000 students. It was inaugurated by late Dr. Marri Chenna Reddy who served as a the Chief Minister of former Andhra Pradesh.

CBIT is the first private engineering college in Telangana.
